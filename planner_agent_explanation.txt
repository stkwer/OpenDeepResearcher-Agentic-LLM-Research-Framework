Planner Agent is responsible for analyzing the user-provided
research topic and breaking it into structured sub-questions.

It creates a clear research roadmap by defining objectives,
sub-questions, and execution steps. This helps guide the
information retrieval and synthesis process in later stages.

In Milestone 1, the Planner Agent takes a topic as input and
outputs a well-organized research plan that can be used by
Searcher and Writer agents.
